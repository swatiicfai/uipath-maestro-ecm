THE PROBLEM

Humanoid RL Training is Chaos

💸

Idle GPU Waste

Teams burn thousands of dollars on cloud GPU instances (AWS, GCP, AMD) that sit completely idle after training finishes.

📉

Silent Divergence

RL runs silently diverge or collapse for hours — reward flatlines, gradients vanish — before anyone notices.

🤖

No Governance

No unified system for anomaly triage, human-in-the-loop approvals, and auditable write-back to cloud registries.
